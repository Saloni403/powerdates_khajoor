Return Policy

Due to the perishable nature of food products, we follow a strict return policy.

    Eligible Returns

    Returns are accepted only if:

  • The product is damaged during transit
  • An incorrect product was delivered
  • The product is expired at the time of delivery
    Non-Eligible Returns
  • Opened or partially consumed products
  • Change of mind
  • Improper storage by the customer

Customers must notify us within 24 hours of delivery along with clear photos/videos for verification.

Exchange Policy

We strive to ensure customer satisfaction and offer exchanges under specific conditions.

    Exchange Conditions
  • Exchange is allowed only for damaged, defective, or incorrect items
  • Request must be raised within 24 hours of delivery
  • Original packaging must be intact

Once verified, a replacement product will be dispatched at no extra cost.

Refund Policy

Refunds are processed only after successful verification of the return or exchange request.

    Refund Method
  • Refunds are credited to the original payment method
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) refunds are processed via bank transfer or UPI
    Refund Timeline
  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after approval

Please note that shipping charges (if applicable) are non-refundable.
